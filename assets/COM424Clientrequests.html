Text: 

You can use what is currently on the website though much of it needs to be updated. Feel free to recommend changes, additions or deletions to what’s there to improve user engagement and experience.[m]

 

Other Visual Elements:  We need more and better imagery on the website including photographs, infographics representing key information[n] and possibly illustrations specific to CRC (See hexagons with illustrations of Polar Bear, etc. in them as an example on Maisha template home page.)
